Trip Advice

Unsure how to plan for your camping trip, backpacking, and any outdoor activity? The Weather Outlet will not only tell you the weather, but what kind of day it would be. For example: It's a HOT DAY! Pack your swim suit and sunscreen. Hikers, be sure to jump into that waterfall for a refreshing cool down. We will provide the ideas to help ease the planning. Relax and have fun!

Scenarios

The Busson Family are planning a trip to Disneyland in October. The weather in that season is hard to figure out what to pack. Our site provides ideas of what could be exspected. This then helps the parents know what to pack and how to plan out their activites.

The Hunkley couple want to plan a New Zealand trip for thier anniversary. The seasons are different, and don't know how to best plan out their trip. Our site will provide information of what the locals do during that time. What to wear, activities available, backup ideas and so forth. This way they are able to plan with ease and not get mobbed by internet information.

Ryan Thomson wants to know surfing conditions in Hawaii. We are able tp provide weather and conditions for such activities. With help from the locals and their data, we can help him plan out his days.
